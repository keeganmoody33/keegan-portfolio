# THE NAME

## Origin: Ludwig Boltzmann's "Lectures on Gas Theory"

### Who Boltzmann Was
- 19th-century physicist
- Pioneered statistical mechanics
- Studied entropy and the behavior of systems at scale
- Worked on understanding how individual particles create macro-level patterns

### "Lectures on Gas Theory"
- His foundational text
- Explained how the behavior of countless individual molecules creates predictable, observable phenomena
- The math of chaos becoming order
- Systems thinking before "systems thinking" was a term

### Why This Resonates
The name captures:
- **Systems thinking**: Understanding how small things aggregate into big patterns
- **Teaching/sharing**: "Lectures" — not just doing, but communicating
- **Scientific rigor**: Grounded in methodology, not just vibes

I love the second law of thermodynamics, entropy — super dope. But let's not over-connect everything to Boltzmann. The connection is the name and the logo (from the book's cover), not a deep philosophical framework across all projects.

**Origin of the name in plain terms:**
> "I can be a guy that will give you a lecture on some stuff. I just can get to rambling. It's kind of funny."

# WHAT LECTURES FROM IS

## The Umbrella

Lectures From is a means to generate and create **software and analog/physical products** that I love.

This isn't about having a bunch of shipped products — it's a vehicle for building things across domains:

- **Software products** — apps, tools, platforms
- **GTM work** — if I need to do go-to-market stuff under this, I can
- **Publishing** — help someone publish a book, handle the design, etc.
- **Music** — get records pressed, create music for myself
- **Physical products** — anything analog that matters

## Why "Lectures From"

### Not Just Building Things — Explaining Them
The "lectures" part matters. It's not just making stuff; it's doing things with the right intention.

### The Philosophy
- Building products that matter
- Trying to add actual value to the people they reach
- Helping people in their creative process and their day-to-day
- Things that challenge the way you think sometimes
- Things that change the way you move

### Connection to Independent Research
From the ASGM fieldwork: It wasn't just data collection — it was giving communities information. Same energy here. Build things that help people.

# WHAT LECTURES FROM IS NOT

## Not a Content Brand
- Not about pumping out LinkedIn posts
- Not building an audience for its own sake
- The work matters more than the visibility

## Not GTM Utilities/Frameworks
- GTM frameworks (like Gutenberg) are **not** part of Lectures From
- That's professional work, separate from this creative/product umbrella

## Not Including Music (Yet)
- Music production is personal, stays in the lab
- "Until I get where I'm going with that"
- Different standards apply — artistic integrity over shipping speed
- People should know I'm passionate about music, but it's not about asking employers to make beats with me

## Not a Traditional Agency
- Not selling services to whoever pays
- The portfolio serves a purpose — building things that matter
